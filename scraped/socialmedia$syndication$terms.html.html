Usage Guidelines

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) provides this syndication service for government, public health, personal, noncommercial, commercial use on a web site, weblog, or similar applications provided that the following guidelines are met.

Guidelines

  1. Links redirect the user to the CDC Web site when the user clicks on them
  2. You do not insert any intermediate page, splash page or other content between the links and the applicable CDC Web site pages
  3. The use or display does not suggest that CDC promotes or endorses any third party causes, ideas, Web sites, products or services
  4. The fundamental meaning of the content contained in the Syndication Content Feeds is not changed or distorted
  5. You do not modify content that is linked to by the Syndication Content Feeds
  6. You may not redistribute the Syndication Content Feed
  7. You must provide attribution to CDC or the relevant Content Feed provider adjacent to the Content Feed
  8. CDC reserves the right to include or apply special codes and to track usage of the Syndication Content Feeds

Note that the Content Syndication site and Content Syndication Feeds are subject to change without notice. If you have any questions about these Usage Guidelines, please contact IMTech@cdc.gov
