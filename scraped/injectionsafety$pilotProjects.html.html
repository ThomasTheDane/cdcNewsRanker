One & Only Campaign Pilot Projects

CDC is currently supporting two state health departments (Nevada and New York) that received funding through the Epidemiology and Laboratory Capacity (ELC) Cooperative Agreement mechanism to pilot test the One & Only Campaign materials including posters and brochures for patients and healthcare providers.
