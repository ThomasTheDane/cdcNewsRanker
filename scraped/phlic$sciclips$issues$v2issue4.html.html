CDC Knowledge to Action Science Clips:

Vol. 2, Issue: 4, 1/25/10
 
Welcome to Knowledge to Action Science Clips, CDC's new weekly digest!
 
Featured this week is a Supplement focusing on American Indians/Alaskan Natives (AI/AN) and indigenous populations that coincides with the 4th Biannual CDC Tribal Consultation session in Atlanta on January 26-28, 2010. In addition to CDC-authored publications and public health articles noted in the media during the past six months, multiple CDC subject matter experts have selected featured articles on health topics relevant to AI/AN and indigenous populations.
 
Knowledge to Action Science Clips is a service of the CDC Public Health Library and Information Center and CDC's Office of the Chief Science Officer.


The Knowledge to Action Science Clips is in the public domain and may be freely forwarded and reproduced without permission. The original sources and the CDC Knowledge to Action Science Clips should be cited as sources.
