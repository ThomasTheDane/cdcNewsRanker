Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) and Pulmonary Embolism (PE) are serious, but preventable medical conditions caused by blood clots that form in a vein. It is important to know about DVT because it can happen to anybody at any age and can cause serious illness, disability, and in some cases, death. The good news is these types of blood clots are preventable and treatable if discovered early.
